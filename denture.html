Denture

Dentures are removable dental appliances used to replace missing teeth. They restore your smile, improve chewing ability, and enhance facial appearance.

Types of Dentures:

  • Full Dentures (complete replacement)
  • Partial Dentures (replace some teeth)

Benefits:

  • Improves appearance
  • Helps in eating and speaking
  • Affordable tooth replacement option
