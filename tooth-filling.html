Tooth Filling

Tooth filling is a procedure used to restore teeth damaged by decay. The decayed portion is removed and replaced with a filling material to restore the tooth’s function and appearance.

Types of Fillings:

  • Composite (tooth-colored)
  • Amalgam (silver)
  • Ceramic fillings

Benefits:

  • Restores tooth strength
  • Prevents further decay
  • Natural appearance
