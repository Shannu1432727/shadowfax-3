Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction is the removal of a tooth that is severely damaged, decayed, or infected. It is done carefully to minimize pain and ensure quick healing.

When is it needed?

  • Severe tooth decay
  • Infection or abscess
  • Wisdom tooth problems

Benefits:

  • Relieves pain
  • Prevents infection spread
  • Improves oral health
