veneers

Porcelain veneers are thin, custom-made shells bonded to the front of teeth to improve their appearance. They help fix chipped, stained, or uneven teeth and give you a bright, confident smile.

Benefits:

  • Improves smile appearance
  • Natural and long-lasting
  • Resistant to stains

Procedure:

  • Tooth preparation
  • Impression and design
  • Bonding veneers to teeth
