Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024. Your privacy matters to us — here is how we protect it.

Summary

Sakthi Dental Clinic is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients and website visitors. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data. We follow all applicable Indian data protection laws and healthcare privacy standards.

1. Who We Are

Sakthi Dental Clinic ("we", "us", "our") is a dental healthcare provider located at 123, Main Road, Hosur, Tamil Nadu – 635 109, India. We operate the website sakthidental.in and provide dental care services to patients in Hosur and the surrounding region.

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact: privacy@sakthidental.in or call +91 98765 43210.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you contact us or book an appointment through our website
  • Medical and dental history, treatment records, X-rays, and clinical notes when you become a patient
  • Insurance details where applicable
  • Any feedback, enquiries, or communications you send us

Information collected automatically when you visit our website:

  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Browser type and version, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation paths
  • Referral source (how you found our website)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Booking, confirming, and managing your dental appointments
  • Providing dental treatment and maintaining accurate clinical records
  • Sending appointment reminders via SMS, email, or WhatsApp (with your consent)
  • Responding to your enquiries and providing customer support
  • Sending follow-up care instructions after treatment
  • Sending occasional newsletters or health tips (only if you have opted in)
  • Improving our website and services through analytics
  • Complying with legal and regulatory healthcare obligations

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Medical Records & Confidentiality

Your medical and dental records are treated with the highest level of confidentiality. We adhere to professional healthcare privacy standards, which means:

  • Clinical information is only accessible to members of your treating dental team
  • Records are stored securely with appropriate access controls
  • We do not share your medical information with third parties without your explicit written consent, except where required by law (e.g. court orders) or in a medical emergency where your life may be at risk
  • We retain patient records for a minimum of 7 years as required by Indian medical regulations, or longer if you remain an active patient

5. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience. The types of cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g. remembering your form inputs). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it. We may use Google Analytics for this purpose. All data is aggregated and anonymised.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings, such as your chosen colour theme (light/dark mode).

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your information with trusted third parties only where necessary:

  • Dental laboratories: When fabricating crowns, dentures, or other dental appliances, we may share necessary information with our laboratory partners. These partners are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Specialist referrals: If we refer you to a specialist, we will share relevant clinical information with their practice with your knowledge.
  • Technology providers: We use secure, reputable software providers for appointment scheduling and patient records. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Our security measures include:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Password-protected and access-controlled patient management systems
  • Regular software updates and security patches
  • Staff training on data protection and confidentiality
  • Restricted physical access to records and computers
  • Regular data backups to prevent loss

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect any misuse of your data, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • Right to correction: If any information we hold is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to have it corrected.
  • Right to deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements for medical records.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent (e.g. marketing communications), you may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Right to data portability: You may request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format where technically feasible.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing for direct marketing purposes at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please email privacy@sakthidental.in or write to us at our clinic address. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

We provide dental care to children and recognise the importance of protecting their privacy. For patients under 18, we collect and process information with the knowledge and consent of a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our website without parental consent.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (such as social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via email. Please review this page periodically.

12. Contact & Complaints

If you have any concerns about how we handle your data, or wish to make a complaint, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@sakthidental.in
  • Phone: +91 98765 43210
  • Post: Sakthi Dental Clinic, 123 Main Road, Hosur, Tamil Nadu – 635 109

We take all complaints seriously and will respond within 30 days. If you remain unsatisfied, you have the right to escalate concerns to the relevant Indian data protection authority.
