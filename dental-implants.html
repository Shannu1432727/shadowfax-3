dental-implants

Dental implants are a permanent solution for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post is placed in the jawbone, which acts as a root and supports a crown, bridge, or denture.

Benefits:

  • Looks and feels like natural teeth
  • Long-lasting solution
  • Improves chewing and speech

Procedure:

  • Implant placement in jawbone
  • Healing period (osseointegration)
  • Crown placement
