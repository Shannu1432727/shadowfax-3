Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is used to treat infection inside the tooth. It removes the infected pulp, cleans the canals, and seals the tooth. This procedure helps save your natural tooth and relieves pain.

Benefits:

  • Stops tooth pain
  • Saves natural tooth
  • Prevents infection spread

Procedure:

  • Cleaning infected pulp
  • Disinfecting canals
  • Sealing and restoring tooth
